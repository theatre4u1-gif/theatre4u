"After 15 years managing our theatre program's inventory on spreadsheets that were out of date the moment I closed them, I built the tool I always wished existed."

— Bob Zick, Founder & CEO · Former Theatre Director, Ocean View High School, Huntington Beach CA

Theatre programs across the country manage hundreds of costumes, props, lighting fixtures, and sound equipment with handwritten lists or nothing at all. Items get lost. Gear gets double-purchased. Valuable equipment sits unused while the school across town rents the same thing from a commercial vendor at full price.

Theatre4u™ solves both problems simultaneously. Programs catalog their inventory with photos and QR-scannable labels. They share items through the Backstage Exchange — a marketplace for free loans, rentals, and sales between programs. The Stage Points rewards system turns every shared item into a benefit for both programs.

Platform Features

Everything your program needs to know what you have — and share what you don't need

📦

Inventory Management

Catalog every costume, prop, lighting fixture, and piece of equipment with photos, conditions, storage locations, tags, and production history. Know exactly what you own at all times.

🔲

QR Code Labels

Every item gets a printable QR label. Scan any item with any phone camera to pull up its full record instantly — condition, location, checkout status, and history. No app download needed.

🏪

Backstage Exchange

List items for rent, sale, or free loan to other programs nearby. Browse what neighboring schools have available. District schools share for free — no platform fee on intra-district transactions.

🎭

Productions Tracker

Create a folder for each show. Assign items from inventory, track what's checked out, and see at a glance what every production needs — from wishlist to opening night.

💰

Funding Tracker

Track grants, Prop 28 allocations, and all program spending. Generate print-ready accountability reports for principals, arts directors, and boards with one click.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Category breakdowns, condition reports, and a Platform Utilization Report designed to hand to a principal or superintendent — showing how Theatre4u protects your program's assets.

📱

Mobile App

Install on iPhone or Android from your browser — no App Store required. Camera integration, QR scanning, and full inventory management from backstage, the costume closet, or the scene shop.

🤖

AI Help

An AI assistant powered by Claude is built into the platform. Ask about inventory management, Prop 28 compliance, or how to use any feature — answered instantly, in plain language.

Stage Points

Your program earns rewards for sharing

Stage Points are Theatre4u's loyalty system. Earn points for adding inventory, completing loans, and referring other programs. Redeem for free months of Pro or discounts on Exchange transactions.
