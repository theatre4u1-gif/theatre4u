Quick Answers

Is there a free plan?
Yes — Theatre4u has a free tier with core inventory features. Pro is $15/month with full access to the Backstage Exchange and all advanced features.
Can I try it before signing up?
Absolutely. Click "Try the Full Demo" on the home page for an interactive walkthrough with sample data — no account needed.
Do you offer district plans?
Yes — District plans cover multiple schools under one subscription starting at $49/month. Select "District Inquiry" above and tell us about your district.
Who owns my data?
You do. Your inventory is yours completely — export it anytime as a CSV. We never sell or share your data.
