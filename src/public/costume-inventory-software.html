Get your costume closet organized in an afternoon

1. Start with your most-used rack. Photograph and log 15–20 pieces — name, size, condition, location. Consistent short sessions beat one marathon.

2. Print QR labels for your tubs and racks so anyone can scan to see what's inside and where it belongs.

3. Tag by show as you go, so production history builds itself and pulling for the next show is effortless.

4. Share what you'll never use again — that period gown set — with programs nearby, free or for a small rental.

Frequently asked questions

Is there free costume inventory software?

Yes — Theatre4u is free to start with up to 25 items, QR labels, and photos, no credit card. Pro is $15/month for unlimited items, reports, and full Backstage Exchange access.

Can I import an existing spreadsheet?

Yes. CSV import brings your existing costume list in at once, so you don't start from scratch.

Does it work on my phone?

Yes — it installs from your browser on iPhone or Android (no app store), with camera capture and QR scanning built in.

Can students help without me losing control?

Yes. Invite team members with roles — crew and stage managers can add and update items while you keep director-level control.
